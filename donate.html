What to do after donating?

The journey doesn't stop after giving a donation. Here's a couple things you can do!

Help volunteer at different organizations! This can be in your local community, or you can participate in well-known movements such as GivingTuesday and DoSomething.org and advocate for the causes you care about!

Research and share more facts about mental health to others who don't know much about. Explain them why mental health is important. If anyone you know has a mental illness and feels alone, let them know that you are there for them and empathize with them.
