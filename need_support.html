Resources

This website is to help others feel warmth and comfort, but in no way does it help diagnose or treat mental illnesses. If you or a loved one are having mental health issues, please refer to these sources.

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Service Administration (SAMHSA) - An agency to provides services for those facing mental or substance-use disorders. There are a different helplines to call ad they are available 24/7.

Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) - The CDC also provides different 24/7 helplines and resources for finding therapists & treatment.

National Alliance on Mental Illnesses (NAMI) - NAMI provides sources on different types of treatments and how to access them. They also provide a helpline here.
