About

Our Purpose

Hello, I create this small website regarding mental health because many of my friends go through mental health issues (depression, anxiety, low self-esteem, etc). I want others to know that they are free to vent and their experiences are valid. I want to create judgement free/positive website for those who can not get it in their personal lives. There are many resources can try going to when your mental health is at a low-point.

As for why I chose HotCoco as the name? I believe that hot cocoa can help warm someone's day. It doesn't have to be hot cocoa. It can be any beverage, any small deed that can make someone feel happy! ☕

Never stop warming up people's hearts
